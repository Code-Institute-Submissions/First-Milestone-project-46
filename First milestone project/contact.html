Here is additional information of the card and its effect
The goal of the game is to try and defeat the enemies nexus, the nexus has a total of 20 hp and takes damage any time a unit attacks and there is no enemy blocker to defend. It can also take damage from spells or different effects on cards. You start off with 1 mana and every round you switch attacking and defending with your opponent and get +1 mana that you can use. So the first round everyone will only have 1 mana to use, the second round 2, third round 3, etc.
