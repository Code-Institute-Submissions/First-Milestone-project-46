Hi and welcome to my very first website!
I made this website because Legends of Runeterra is a game that I enjoy playing at times in my free time but sometimes im left to wonder "what kind of deck should I make?
But now you don't need to worry about that anymore! Just put it which regions you want to include (max of 2)
Put in what kind of playstyle you would like (aggro = fast, shorter games, midrange = games where you overpower the enemy with strong units, control = you stall out the enemy with spells
Last but not least pick how many champions you would like your deck to include (from 0 to 6) and the randomizer will give you a deck youve never seen!
